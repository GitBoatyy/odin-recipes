Simple Lasagna Recipe

Here I will show you a simple Lasagna recipe that came up first on google

ENJOY

This recipe has been passed down for generations because lasagna is all basically the same. You got some noodles, you got some meat, you got some sauce, you got some cheese.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta 10 cups
  • Cheddar 1 oz
  • Eggs 4 dozen
  • Meat. Any kind its all meat. lots of.
  • Tomato sauce, between 1 and infitity cans. do your like your lasagna as soup?
  • Onions all of.
  • Garlic qty 4
  • Pasta, the lasagna kind

Instructions

Cheese filling

  1. Mix the ricotta and cheddar
  2. Add 1 dozen eggs and mix
  3. Add all of the garlic and onion
  4. Stop, realize you added too much onion
  5. Scoop some onion out with your hands before opening the garbage.
  6. Dump cheese covered egg-onion in sink and forget about it
  7. Set aside cheese filling

Meat Sauce

  1. Take meat. Add sauce
  2. Add onion and garlic
  3. Put in pot on high
  4. Burn it
  5. Say "the brown bits is all the flavor"

Make the lasagna

  1. Grab a much to big or much too small dish
  2. Layer the meat sauce, cheese filling, and noodles in that order
  3. Repeat cheese filling and meet sauce recipe until you have enough to make a lasagna for 5 people even though only you will be eating this for the next 3 weeks
  4. Bake on like 450 or something for like a hour.
  5. When you smell smoke take the lasagna outside and forget about it
  6. Shoo away birds and rodents once lasagna has cooled and you have convinced the fire department you are ok
